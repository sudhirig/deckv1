## The Five-Act Structure in Detail

### Act 1: The Hook & Problem
**Objective:** Create urgency and establish market opportunity

1. **Title Slide:** Complete pitch in one line - "Agentic AI • Tax Alpha • Alternative Access • GIFT City Gateway"
2. **AI Hook:** Stanford study showing 600% AI outperformance - third-party validation before self-promotion
3. **Switching Epidemic:** 46% of HNWIs firing advisors - "Why Now?" with massive opportunity
4. **Alpha Gap:** Three specific failures quantified - sets up our three alpha agents

### Act 2: The Solution
**Objective:** Showcase product and unique value proposition

5. **Hero Product:** Live platform screenshots with "100% OPERATIONAL TODAY" badge
6. **Agentic Architecture:** Visual comparison old bottleneck vs new AI swarm model
7. **Three Alpha Agents:** Direct answers to Act 1 problems with quantified impact

### Act 3: The Deep Dive
**Objective:** Demonstrate technical depth and competitive moats

8-10. **India Gateway Deep-Dive:** Problem → GIFT City solution → AI agent enabler
11. **Tax Alpha Agent:** Daily vs annual TLH showing +1.8% alpha ($180K on $10M)
12. **Alt Asset Agent:** AI-generated deal memos unlocking private markets
13. **Full Architecture:** 20+ agent swarm with technical stack details

### Act 4: The Proof
**Objective:** Validate with traction and competitive positioning

14. **User Journeys:** Three personas with specific dollar outcomes ($142K, $85K, $1.5M)
15. **Live Platform:** Real Zerodha integration, real portfolio, production stack
16. **Competitive Matrix:** AI Capability vs India Access - we win both dimensions
17. **Triple Lock Moat:** Technical (2 years), Regulatory (18 months), Trust (broker-agnostic)

### Act 5: The Business & Ask
**Objective:** Present investment opportunity and close

18. **Market Sizing:** Animated TAM/SAM/SOM circles - $112T to $12.5M
19. **ROI Calculator:** Interactive proof of 8-10x value creation
20. **Growth Projection:** Path to $1B AUM with unit economics
21. **Team:** Expertise across AI, finance, compliance with traction markers
22. **The Ask:** Bold $5M seed round with clear milestones
23. **Use of Funds:** 40% AI, 30% Integration, 30% Growth with 18-month roadmap
24. **The Close:** Inevitability statement with aspiration and call-to-action
25. **Appendix:** Navigation to backup slides for deep-dive Q&A
